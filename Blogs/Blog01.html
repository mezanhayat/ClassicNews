Squid Game Season 3: Release Date, Rumors, and What to Expect

Last updated: June 30, 2025

Since its explosive debut in 2021, Squid Game has become Netflix’s most-watched series, captivating audiences with its brutal survival drama and social commentary. After the success of Season 2, fans are already buzzing about Season 3. Will it happen? When can we expect it? What new deadly games and twists await?

In this blog, we’ll dive into:

  • Official confirmation and expected release date for Squid Game Season 3
  • Potential plotlines and returning characters
  • New cast members and possible game twists
  • Fan theories and behind-the-scenes updates

1. Has Squid Game Season 3 Been Confirmed?

As of June 2024, Netflix has not officially announced Squid Game Season 3. However, given the massive success of the first two seasons, a third installment is highly likely.

  • Season 2 was confirmed in June 2022 and released in late 2024/early 2025.
  • Creator Hwang Dong-hyuk initially planned Squid Game as a standalone series but expanded it due to demand.
  • Netflix CEO Ted Sarandos has hinted at the franchise’s long-term potential, possibly including spin-offs.

Expected timeline:

  • Season 3 announcement in late 2024/early 2025
  • Filming in 2025
  • Release in late 2026 or early 2027

2. What Could Squid Game Season 3 Be About?

A. Gi-hun’s Revenge Against the VIPs

Season 2’s ending likely sets up Gi-hun going after the wealthy elites behind the games. Will he infiltrate their circle? Start a rebellion?

B. A New Set of Deadly Games

The Front Man may recruit fresh players, possibly from other countries. Could we see international contestants? Rumors suggest a US version of the games.

C. The Origins of the Squid Game

More backstory on Il-nam (Player 001) and how the games began. Potential flashbacks to early versions of the competition.

3. Returning & New Cast Members

Likely Returning Characters:

  • Seong Gi-hun (Lee Jung-jae)
  • The Front Man (Lee Byung-hun)
  • Hwang Jun-ho (Wi Ha-joon)

Potential New Additions:

  • Global contestants (possibly including Hollywood actors)
  • New VIPs and game hosts

4. New Twists & Games We Might See

The first two seasons featured horrifying yet iconic games. What could Season 3 introduce?

  • Korean Jacks (Gonggi) – A childhood game turned deadly
  • The Ladder Game – A life-or-death climb with traps
  • The Maze – A Hunger Games-style survival challenge

Other ideas include:

  • Higher cash prize (possibly 100 billion won)
  • More psychological manipulation (e.g., betrayal of loved ones)

5. Fan Theories & Predictions

Theory 1: Gi-hun Becomes the New Front Man

Some fans believe Gi-hun might take over the games to dismantle them from within.

Theory 2: The Games Go Global

Could there be multiple Squid Game locations worldwide?

Theory 3: A Prequel Season

Exploring Young Il-nam’s rise and the first-ever Squid Game.

6. When Can We Expect a Trailer?

  • Teaser in mid-2026
  • Full trailer in late 2026
  • Release in early 2027

7. Final Thoughts: Will Season 3 Happen?

While nothing is confirmed yet, Netflix would be foolish to cancel such a hit show. Given the unresolved cliffhangers and fan demand, Squid Game Season 3 is almost inevitable.
