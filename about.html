About Classic Times

Our Story, Mission, and Team

Our Story

Founded in 2020, Classic Times emerged as a non-profit news organization dedicated to preserving the integrity of journalism in an era of misinformation. What began as a small team of passionate journalists has grown into a family of 10-20 dedicated professionals committed to delivering unbiased, fact-based reporting.

In our early days, we operated from a modest workspace with a simple mission: to provide news without agenda. Today, we reach thousands of readers daily while maintaining that same commitment to journalistic excellence.

Our Mission

Truth

To uncover and report facts with accuracy and context, without fear or favor.

Integrity

To maintain the highest ethical standards in all our journalistic practices.

Community

To serve our readers with news that matters to their lives and communities.

Our Team

Classic Times is powered by a diverse team of 10-20 journalists, editors, researchers, and support staff who bring a wealth of experience and perspectives to our newsroom. As a non-profit, every team member is driven by passion rather than profit, united by our shared commitment to public service journalism.
